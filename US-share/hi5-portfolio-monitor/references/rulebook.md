# Hi5 rulebook and version boundaries

## Current public rule

- Universe: IWY, RSP, SPMO, VNQ, PFF. MOAT was replaced by SPMO in August 2025.
- Add about USD 10,000, equally across five ETFs. After reported total value reaches USD 1 million, the Sheet says one add becomes 1% of total value.
- First monthly add: first day RSP falls at least 1%; otherwise the third Friday.
- Second monthly add: after the first, when RSP month-to-date decline reaches 5%.
- Optional third add: “人性之极”; the public rule lacks a fully reproducible threshold.
- Maximum three adds per month; rebalance to equal weights each August.

## Historical boundaries

- 2023 launch period included discretionary skips in November and December.
- The 2024 public rule used the capitalization-weighted S&P 500 trigger, not RSP.
- The author stated RSP became the trigger from January 2025.
- MOAT changed to SPMO in August 2025.

Use actual transactions for historical results. Use the current rule only for forward monitoring. Label the third-add rule non-replicable until a timestamped mechanical definition exists.

## Campaign labels

- `initial`: 2023-08-03 initial allocation.
- `rebalance`: 2024-08-01 and 2025-08-01 portfolio rebalances.
- `recurring`: all other five-ETF add campaigns.

Do not pool initial/rebalance transactions into the primary three-day follower statistic.
